The School:

At Magitnup's Dance School, we believe it's important to teach more than just steps. We want to ignite a passion for dance, foster creativity, confidence and instill a strong sense of belonging within our students. Our experienced and passionate instructors bring expertise from various dance disciplines, ensuring a dynamic and enriching learning environment.

Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps, or an experienced dancer honing your skills, we welcome everyone to discover the magic of dancing. From classical ballet to contemporary styles, hip-hop to ballroom, we provide the perfect space for dancers to explore the melodious and rhythmical world of movement.

Explore our classes, witness inspiring performances, and tell your story. Step by step. We hope to welcome you into our dance school where our students can find a second family and home.
